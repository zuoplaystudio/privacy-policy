Privacy Policy - 新春球

Effective Date: December 29, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the 新春球 app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices created by zuoplay.studio (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address), operating system, and usage patterns within the Application.

Location Information: The Application does not request or access your precise GPS location from your device. We may infer your general region (e.g., country or city level) based on your IP address solely for advertising purposes. This helps our third-party ad providers deliver more relevant content based on your general region.

Transparency Commitment: This Application does not request, access, or collect your name, contacts, call logs, text messages, microphone, or camera data. The Application does not collect your device ID (except for the Advertising ID used for ad tracking).

Photo Library Access: The Application may request permission to access your device's photo library only when you explicitly choose to use the "Save Image" or "Screenshot" features within the Application. This access is strictly limited to saving the generated content to your local device. We do not upload, collect, or share your personal photos or any content from your gallery to our servers.

Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The Application utilizes Artificial Intelligence technologies to enhance user experience. Please be advised that the artistic assets and visual elements within the Application are generated using AI during the development phase.

The AI technologies are primarily used for asset creation. No user data is collected or processed by AI during runtime for model training purposes.

Third Party Access & Data Sharing

The Service Provider will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. The Application utilizes third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase

Advertising-related data is governed by Google AdMob's policy. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertising in your device's OS settings.

Your Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA/PDPA)

In accordance with global standards (including GDPR, CCPA, and Taiwan's PDPA), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access & Review: Request to access or review the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Copy: Request a portable copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Request to supplement or correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Cease Processing: Request to stop the collection, processing, or use of your personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on automated decision-making (including AI systems).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at zuoplay.studio@gmail.com. Please include "Privacy Policy Data Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 15 to 30 days.

Data Retention & Opt-Out

The Application temporarily stores necessary data locally. Server-side storage consists of anonymous analytics. If the Application is inactive for 24 months, we will delete associated anonymous data.

Data Deletion Path: Since the Application does not require a user account to function, users can stop all information collection and terminate the data collection process at any time by simply uninstalling the Application from their device. For specific requests regarding the erasure of past anonymous data, please contact us via the email provided below.

Security

We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Data is encrypted in transit using standard industry protocols (such as HTTPS) to ensure that your information remains secure while being transmitted from your device to our service partners. However, please be aware that no security system can prevent all potential breaches.

Children's Privacy

The Service Provider is committed to protecting the privacy of children. The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age threshold in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data without your consent, please contact us at zuoplay.studio@gmail.com. We will take immediate steps to verify the request and delete such information from our servers.

We encourage parents to monitor their children's mobile usage to help ensure a safe digital environment.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Application is deemed approval of all changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at: zuoplay.studio@gmail.com. We will respond within 15 to 30 business days.

This privacy policy applies exclusively to 新春球 by zuoplay.studio.
